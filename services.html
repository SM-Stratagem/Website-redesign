Method

How an engagement
actually runs.

i
Phase 01

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, current-state audit, and a written brief everyone can sign off on before a line of code is written.

ii
Phase 02

Map

Translate the brief into architecture: information, data, identity. We design the spine before any interface or query.

iii
Phase 03

Build

Sprint cycles with shippable increments. Weekly demos. You feel progress instead of waiting for a reveal at the end.

iv
Phase 04

Scale

Hand-off with documentation, telemetry, and a 60-day stabilization window. The work keeps working after we step away.
